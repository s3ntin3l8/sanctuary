Document Extraction Engine

Choose how PDFs are converted to markdown during ingest. Chandra uses the active OCR instance and produces much cleaner text on stamped, scanned, or image-heavy documents; Docling runs locally with Tesseract and is faster on clean digital PDFs. Other file types (DOCX, EML, TXT) always use Docling.

Workers · AI Concurrency

How many AI tasks (document summaries, enrichment, embeddings) the ai worker runs in parallel against your model server.
