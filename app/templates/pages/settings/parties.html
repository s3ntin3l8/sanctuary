User Context

Prepended to every AI system prompt. Describe your role and goals — not your name or lawyers (those are handled by Party Identity above).

Example: "I am the respondent in a family court custody dispute (Sorgerechtsverfahren). I am seeking joint custody and regular contact with my children. My main concerns are countering unfounded allegations, demonstrating stable living conditions, and not missing any procedural deadlines. I prefer fact-based, measured responses over aggressive tactics."

Settable as AI_USER_CONTEXT in .env; the value below overrides env when non-empty.

How this works

  • Your name and lawyers are injected into every AI extraction call as authoritative ground truth for document classification.
  • Courts, Verfahrensbeistand, Jugendamt, and experts are automatically classified — you don't need to list them.
  • Opposing parties are per-case — manage them from the case dashboard parties panel. The AI auto-suggests opposing parties after the first case brief runs.
  • User Context is different from Party Identity: it frames your role and goals, not who you are by name. It shapes how the AI interprets facts and writes briefs.
  • After saving Party Identity, re-run the case brief to update parties. To fix existing document origins, use "Re-enrich" on the case dashboard.
